Our Passion

PC Partstore Commerce, Inc., headquartered in the City of Industry, California, is a leading e-commerce company offering direct sales and an online marketplace platform for IT computer components, consumer electronics, entertainment, smart home and gaming products and provides certain third-party logistics services globally.

About Us

Over the course of two decades, PC Partstore has built a highly reputable name in the tech-focused e-commerce market. Today, our mix of consumer and business solutions has earned us a place as one of the most dynamic platforms in North America—and we’re just getting started.
